About this system

This educational Laboratory Sample Tracking System is a compact LIMS-style app designed for small academic labs and database coursework.

  • Entities · researchers, samples, experiments, storage locations, and results.
  • Relationships · many-to-many between samples and experiments, plus foreign-key links from results for full traceability.
  • Queries · multi-table joins (traceability) and aggregate views (storage summary).

Use the top navigation to manage entities, or the Queries menu to demonstrate joins, filters, and aggregates in a realistic lab scenario.
